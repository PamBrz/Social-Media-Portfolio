Social Media Analysis: Instagram

UI and UX Design: always adjusting to new trends, very personalized algorithm

Features and functionalities: like, comment, DMs, tagging, posting, live-streams, donations, shops for businesses, following, analytics, verification, polls, close friends story

Target audience, user demographics: mainly young people in the beginning, now everyone

Monetization strategies: influencers, advertisement

Privacy and security measures: report, block, no comments, private account / Bots, tracking data, Data privacy meta

Recent updates and innovations: shared posts, notes, threads, broadcasting channels, paid “premium” content
